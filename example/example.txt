The general rules are:
1. Remove all advertisements and sponsored content
2. Remove navigation elements like table of contents
3. Remove footers and related articles sections
4. Keep the main content and headings
5. Remove any social media links or sharing buttons

Here is an example:

Input text:
```
# Recipe Blog

ADVERTISEMENT

## Navigation
- Home
- Recipes
- About

## How to Bake Cookies
These cookies are amazing!

SPONSORED: Check out our baking tools!

### Instructions
1. Mix ingredients
2. Bake at 350°F

---
Related Posts | Share on Facebook | Subscribe
```

Should be cleaned to:
```
# Recipe Blog

## How to Bake Cookies
These cookies are amazing!

### Instructions
1. Mix ingredients
2. Bake at 350°F
```
